How to understand the multiplication in the real-life


Description of multiplication

a mathematical operation, symbolized by a × b, a ⋅ b, a ∗ b, or ab, and signifying, \n when a and b
are positive integers, that a is to be added to itself as many times as there are units in b; the
addition of a number to itself as often as is indicated by another number, as in 2×3 or 5×10.
